09 10 Key Insights from the Data
📈
Fear is the Sweet Spot for Longs
BUY trades during Fear generated $2.31M — 2.88× more than buying during Greed. The data is unambiguous: dip-buying fear periods outperforms momentum chasing.
🎯
Sell in Greed = #1 Strategy
SELL during Greed yielded $4.06M — the single best combo. Profit factor of 11.0 during Extreme Greed means for every $1 lost, $11 was won.
⚡
Extreme Greed = Highest Avg Trade PnL
$74.74 average PnL per trade during Extreme Greed vs $34.72 in Extreme Fear. Momentum is real — but it works better for sellers than buyers.
🔄
Negative Correlation (PnL vs Sentiment)
Daily PnL correlates -0.083 with sentiment. Feb–Apr 2025 saw record PnL ($5.87M in 3 months) while sentiment was in Fear/Extreme Fear territory.
💰
Limit Orders Beat Market Orders
Limit orders (Crossed=false) average $80 PnL/trade vs $36 for market orders. Patient execution is rewarded — especially during Fear periods.
🕛
12:00 IST is Peak Profit Hour
Noon IST trades average $131 PnL — 2.7× the baseline. 7 AM and 7–8 PM IST are also strong windows. Midnight and late night are worst.
📉
Biggest Losses Happen in Greed
7 out of 10 worst trading days occurred during Greed/Extreme Greed sentiment. Apr 23 2025 alone lost $419K on a 72-score Greed day.
🪙
@107 Defies the Pattern
Unlike BTC/ETH/SOL/HYPE (all peak in Fear), @107 is most profitable during Extreme Greed ($1.99M). Asset-specific sentiment sensitivity matters.
📊
Larger Positions Amplify Sentiment Edge
Q4 (XLarge) positions during Extreme Greed average $242 PnL vs $5 for Q1 (Small). Sizing up on high-conviction contrarian signals amplifies returns.
🔒
Sentiment is Sticky — Trade the Regime
Fear persists 610+ days in a row, Greed 498. Sentiment rarely flips overnight. Regime-following (not day-trading emotion shifts) is more effective.
10 Data-Driven Trading Strategy Recommendations
STRATEGY 01
The Contrarian Core: Buy Fear, Sell Greed
When the Fear & Greed Index drops below 35 (Fear/Extreme Fear), increase long exposure on BTC, ETH, SOL, HYPE. These assets show consistent outperformance during fear regimes. Conversely, when the index rises above 70, reduce longs and open shorts or take profits. The data shows selling in greed returns 5× more than buying. Use limit orders (Crossed=false) — they outperform market orders by 2.2× on average.
STRATEGY 02
Time-Weighted Execution: Trade the 12PM–1PM & 7PM–8PM IST Windows
The data reveals 12:00–13:00 IST averages $131 PnL/trade — the highest of any hour. The 19:00–20:00 IST window is second-best at $55. Avoid placing large trades between 22:00–00:00 IST where avg PnL drops to $18–19. Batch your highest-conviction trades into these windows for maximum efficiency.
STRATEGY 03
Asset-Specific Sentiment Allocation
Not all assets respond equally to sentiment. Trade @107 during Extreme Greed — it generates $1.99M in that regime and actually loses during Extreme Fear. For BTC, ETH, SOL, HYPE: maximum allocation during Fear conditions. Build a two-bucket portfolio: "Fear Assets" (BTC/ETH/SOL/HYPE) and "Greed Assets" (@107-type momentum plays). Rotate allocation based on the daily index reading.
STRATEGY 04
Size Up on Conviction: Q4 Positions in High-Signal Conditions
XLarge (Q4) positions average $242 PnL in Extreme Greed and $174 in Fear — 3–4× the return of small positions. The data supports increasing position size when sentiment aligns with your thesis (e.g., large long during Fear, large short during Greed). Avoid large positions in Extreme Fear without a clear catalyst — Q4 returns $119 vs $173 in regular Fear.
STRATEGY 05
Regime Persistence: Don't Fight the Sentiment Trend
Sentiment regimes last weeks, not days. Fear persists 610+ consecutive days on average; Greed 498. This means once the index enters a zone, you have time to position. Don't anticipate reversals too early. Wait for confirmed transitions (consecutive days changing regime) before flipping strategy. The best traders in this dataset maintained consistent strategy within sentiment regimes rather than day-trading the index.
